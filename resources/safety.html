Where Are the Gaps?

These are build opportunities. Things that don't exist yet, or don't exist well enough.

Intersectional Safety Dashboard

No tool maps safety across all 6 dimensions for one person. A woman could be physically safe but economically trapped. Nothing connects these dots in real time.

All 6 Dimensions Personalized

Financial Escape Planning Tool

99% of DV involves financial abuse but almost no apps help women secretly build financial independence while still in unsafe situations. Credit building, hidden savings, job prep.

Economic Physical

AI-Powered Legal Navigator for DV

Legal systems are overwhelming. No app uses AI to walk a survivor through protection orders, custody, housing rights in plain language, step by step, jurisdiction-specific.

Structural Economic

Community Safety Network (Not Just SOS)

Every physical safety app assumes you need police. What about communities where police aren't safe? Peer-based safety networks with check-ins, local safe houses, trusted people.

Physical Structural

Deepfake Shield for Everyday Women

Detection tools exist but are built for enterprises. No simple, free tool lets a woman check if her image is being used in synthetic media and auto-submit takedowns.

Digital Psychological

Reproductive Data Vault

Post-Dobbs, period tracker data is being subpoenaed. No app offers truly sovereign, on-device-only reproductive health tracking where women own and control 100% of their data.

Reproductive Digital

Workplace Microaggression Tracker

AI tools detect bias in job postings but nothing helps women document and pattern-match daily workplace experiences. Private, timestamped, legally useful documentation.

Structural Psychological

Culturally Specific Safety Resources

Most safety apps are built for English-speaking Western women. Huge gap for multilingual, culturally contextualized safety tools for immigrant, Indigenous, and Global South women.

All 6 Dimensions Accessibility

Post-Trauma Financial Recovery AI

After leaving an abusive situation, women face destroyed credit, employment gaps, housing barriers. No AI tool provides a personalized recovery roadmap integrating financial, legal, and emotional rebuilding.

Economic Psychological

Safety-Centered Dating Intelligence

Noonlight integrates with Tinder, but no tool helps women research a date's public records, verify identity, or share safety plans with friends, all in one private flow.

Physical Digital

Housing Safety Checker

No AI tool helps women evaluate rental/housing safety: neighborhood data, landlord reviews, structural features (locks, lighting, exits), proximity to resources, all through a safety lens.

Physical Structural

Girls' Early Intervention Platform

Almost every tool targets adult women. Nothing uses AI to help girls (ages 10-17) develop safety literacy, recognize early warning signs, and build digital and emotional resilience before crisis.
