Privacy Policy

Effective: March 27, 2026  |  Last updated: March 27, 2026

AIdedEQ and The Practice Center ("we," "us," or "our") believe transparency is a practice, not a checkbox. This policy describes what information we collect through our website at aidedeq.org and related tools, how we use it, who we share it with, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

We collect only what we need, and nothing more:

  • Contact form submissions — Your name, email address, area of interest, and any message you choose to share. This is submitted voluntarily through our contact and waitlist forms.
  • Basic analytics — Anonymized, aggregate data about how visitors use our site (pages visited, general location by country/region, device type). We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not track individuals across the web.
  • Cookies — We use only essential cookies required for the site to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party behavioral tracking.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever.
  • We do not use third-party advertising networks.
  • We do not build behavioral profiles or track you across other websites.
  • We do not collect sensitive personal data (race, health, financial information) through this website.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to you — When you submit a contact form, we use your information to reply to your inquiry.
  • To send updates you requested — If you join a waitlist or express interest in a tool or program, we may send relevant updates. You can opt out at any time by emailing us.
  • To improve our tools — Aggregate, anonymized analytics help us understand which tools and resources people find most useful.

Who We Share Information With

We share your information only with the services necessary to operate this website:

  • Netlify — Hosts our website and processes form submissions. Netlify's Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Provides the typefaces used on this site. Google may collect basic connection data (IP address) when fonts are loaded. Google's Privacy Policy

We do not share your information with data brokers, advertisers, or any other third parties.

How We Protect Your Information

  • Our site is served over HTTPS (encrypted connection) at all times.
  • Form submissions are processed through Netlify's secure infrastructure with encrypted data handling.
  • We limit access to personal data to authorized team members who need it to respond to inquiries.
  • We regularly review our practices and update them as needed.

Our Tools

Some tools linked from this site (such as anchorED, WellBEing Snapshot, LoreDrop, and others) are hosted on separate platforms and may have their own data practices. When you leave aidedeq.org to use an external tool, that tool's own privacy policy applies. We encourage you to review the privacy practices of any tool before entering personal information.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Our youth-serving programs (such as GirlsWhoVibe) have separate, age-appropriate data practices with appropriate parental/guardian consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of any personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct — Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete — Ask us to delete your personal information.
  • Opt out — Unsubscribe from any communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@aidedeq.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our tools and practices evolve. When we make changes, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. For significant changes, we'll note them here.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? We're happy to explain anything in plain language.
